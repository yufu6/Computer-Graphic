CSE 470 HW#2: Pick a Cube

Author: Yu Fu              Date: Feb 22 2021
Description: Eight cubes are centered at equally spaced positions on the circle, and one cube is centered at the origin. The outer cubes are rotated around their centers and the axis vectors defined by their respective centers and the origin. The user can interact with speed, size, and selection.
Functionality:
The eight squares lie on a circle of radius 0.75 and rotate in different directions. The center square does not move. The user can click on one of the eight squares and the center square should rotate with the square clicked by the user. The user can also adjust the speed of rotation of the cube with respect to the size of the cube
Parameters:
mvMatrix: define the cube matrix. Other mvMatrix used for define every cube with different location and rotation.

sclare: define the scale of the cude. The origianl scale is 0.2, 0.2, 0.2

t: translate formula, used for adjust the cube.

theta: the degree of rotation speed. Used for change rotation speed also.
